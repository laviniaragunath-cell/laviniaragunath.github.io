About Me

Who I am

I'm a freshman at NYU Stern studying Business, Technology, and Entrepreneurship. Originally from Weinheim, Germany, I grew up between cultures and languages — a background that shapes how I think about problems and people.

Outside of academics, I run, travel when I can, and lose myself in crime novels. I'm drawn to the details: in a story, a city, a design, or a pitch deck.

I'm interested in how technology reshapes industries — and in being someone who helps shape it rather than just watch it happen.
